Shankarananda Kalakshetra

Shankarananda Kalakshetra is a premier centre in Hyderabad for the classical arts.

Students are trained in the practical aspects of Bharatanatyam, its theory, talam, history, legend and myths , and philosophy . Senior students are also taught, Nattuvangam and choreography. Other course offers include an annual summer intensive, invitations to performances, and exposure to world-class artists and performances.

Admissions : Students will only be admitted into the program with the approval of the Artistic Director. Traditionally, new students join on Vijayadasami, an auspicious day in the Hindu calendar. Admissions are accepted thrice a year : July, Vijayadashami day of every year, and January. Minimum age for joining is 7 years of age.
